    Mass Creator Help

    With the Mass Creator, you can create many flashcards easily and simultaneously.

    On each line, enter a question and answer, separated by some token (this is the entry style). Each line will be converted into a flashcard.

    Example (free-reponse flashcards):

    2+2: 4
    Color of the sky: Blue

    Multiple-choice flashcards

    To make multiple-choice flashcards, you need special formatting in your answer. Separate each answer choice with "|" and put a "*" in front of the right answer choice. You can have as many answer choices as you want, but only one can be right. The entry style can still be anything.

    Example (open-ended flashcards):

    3+3 / 5|*6|7
    Color of the sun / *Yellow|Pink|Blue|Silver

      Study from multiple devices? Set up sync so your flashcards are automatically synchronized between your computer, phone, tablet, or anything else!

      Think up a unique, memorable passphrase consisting of 3-4 words (see the examples below) and you can start syncing. Then enter your passphrase again on another device to connect it to sync.

      If you've set up sync from another device, just enter your passphrase here and you'll be hooked up.

      Start syncing

      Passcode Examples

      A good passcode will be unique, memorable, and preferably 3-4 words long. Quotes, movie titles, and names work well. Here are some examples:

      • boston red sox
      • shaken not stirred
      • finding nemo
      • franklin delano roosevelt

      Privacy

      Cabra cares about your privacy! It's not developed by some creepy company out to get you (and your money) - but instead by a student just like you.

      Your data, therefore, is stored securely and anonymously in the cloud. Besides your passcode, Cabra doesn't keep any data on you - why should we care?

      Got a privacy question? Please don't hesitate to write to me!
